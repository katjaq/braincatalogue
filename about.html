Brain Catalogue

About Blog

Our aim is to celebrate the diversity of the vertebrate brain by making high quality data, open and freely available to everyone. Do you have data that you would like to share? Do not hesitate to contact us!

The Brain Catalogue is developed by Roberto Toro, researcher at the Neuroscience Department of the Institut Pasteur; Marc Herbin, curator of the Vertebrate Brain Collection of the Jardin des Plantes from where many of our specimens come from; and Mathieu Santin, researcher at the CENIR laboratory at the Institut du Cerveau et de la Moelle (ICM) where most of the MRI scanning is performed. Scientific, methodological and financial support has been prodived by Florencia Grisanti (Taxidermy Workshop of the Natural History Museum in Paris), Alexandra Petiet (CENIR laboratory) and Olivier Colliot (head of the Cogimage team at the ICM).
